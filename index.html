THE SEA MONKEY PROJECT

Empowering communities and growing education to create positive social and environmental impact for our Oceans.

Who are We?

At The Sea Monkey Project, we turn trash into treasure, creating sustainable livelihoods and fostering a decentralized circular economy ecosystem, one community at a time. We believe that by rethinking our material choices and equipping marginalized groups with the tools and knowledge to upcycle plastic waste, we can make a lasting impact on both the environment and society. Together, we are building a future where the oceans are cleaner, and opportunities are endless.

Our Story

Our story isn’t just about combating plastic pollution; it’s about building a movement that reshapes how we think about waste and sustainability. In Malaysia, where the impact of plastic pollution is particularly severe, The Sea Monkey Project took on the challenge of laying the groundwork for an upcycling ecosystem that didn’t exist before. We didn’t just stop at designing and building upcycling machines; we created the infrastructure necessary for a sustainable recycling ecosystem. Through hands-on training and support, we empowered local communities, businesses, and organizations to manage their plastic waste effectively. Our efforts have been the catalyst for a broader recycling movement in Malaysia, setting a roadmap that others can follow.

OUR IMPACT

The Sea Monkey Project’s impact extends far beyond just building upcycling machines. We have been instrumental in creating a self-sustaining recycling ecosystem in Malaysia and empowering communities worldwide to manage their plastic waste. Our work extends across a diverse range of industries and sectors, thanks to the strong partnerships we've built with clients around the world. From global corporations to grassroots organizations, The Sea Monkey Project works hand-in-hand with multiple clients to drive sustainable solutions that combat plastic waste.
